Summer Music Festival

Central Park, New York

May 25, 2025

7:00 PM - 11:00 PM

Price: $59.99

About This Event

Join us for an unforgettable night of music at the Summer Music Festival! This annual event brings together top artists and bands from around the world for a night of incredible performances under the stars.

The Summer Music Festival is Central Park's premier music event, featuring multiple stages, food vendors, and interactive experiences. Whether you're a die-hard music fan or just looking for a fun night out, this festival has something for everyone.

Featured Artists

  • The Resonant Echo (Headliner)
  • Electric Pulse
  • Midnight Serenade
  • Acoustic Harmony
  • Urban Beats Collective

What to Expect

Gates open at 5:00 PM, with performances beginning at 7:00 PM. The event will feature:

  • 3 stages with continuous performances
  • Food and beverage vendors
  • Merchandise booths
  • VIP lounge areas (for VIP ticket holders)
  • Interactive art installations

Important Information

  • This is an all-ages event
  • Outside food and drinks are not permitted
  • Bring a valid ID for age verification if purchasing alcohol
  • Limited seating available; feel free to bring blankets
  • Event will proceed rain or shine
